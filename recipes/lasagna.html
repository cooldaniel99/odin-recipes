Lasagna

The wonderful world of lasagna

This is lasagna. Isn't it beautiful?? We believe so. Enjoy.

It is made of lasagna noodles, freshly made with eggs, water, and flour. A meat sauce with many choices of herbs and flavors. Ricotta cheese, or if you like, maybe even some mozzerella. Then even more cheese on the top.

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Meat (We chose 93% lean turkey meat)
  • Salt, pepper, oregano, onion powder, garlic powder
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded parm
  1. Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water until loose
  2. Place a layer of noodles on the bottom of the pan
  3. Add a layer of sauce/meat
  4. Add a layer of cheese (ricotta)
  5. Then add a smaller layer of sauce again
  6. Add the shredded parm on top, then repeat the steps 3-4 times depending on dish size
  7. Then cover in tin-foil and bake on 375 for 1 hour
