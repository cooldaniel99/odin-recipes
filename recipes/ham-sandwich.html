Ham Sandwich

The wonderful world of Ham Sammy

Ahh.. sandwiches. Best of all a ham sandwich. Grilled, not grilled. They are always good.

  • Bread (wheat for this example)
  • Cheese (american in this case)
  • Ham (any kind, we chose Smoked Honey Ham)
  • Mayo (dukes of course)
  • Salt & Pepper
  1. First take the bread and spread the dukes mayo on it evenly
  2. Then take a pinch of salt and pepper and sprinkle onto both mayo covered sides of bread
  3. Take the slice of cheese and put it on top of the mayo
  4. Take and fold the ham in half on top of the cheese
  5. You can place another slide of cheese on the other half of bread if you're feeling adventurous
  6. Finally put it all together, grab a sharp knife, and slide in two
  7. Enjoy!
